What is this?

The purpose of this application is to display data pertaining to the second-generation project for Find Your City, an application where you can learn, compare, and receive recommendations on cities across the United States! Within this Flask API, we will display multiple facets within the data which one could take into account when searching for their particular city which include, but, are not limited to statistics concerning: Heart Disease (leading cause of death), COVID-19 cases and deaths, and many population statistics.
